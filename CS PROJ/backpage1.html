  • LumadUprise is dedicated to advocating for the rights, land preservation, and protection of the Lumad people. The Lumad, indigenous communities in the Philippines, are facing critical threats to their existence due to ongoing violence and displacement. Our organization stands as a beacon of hope and resistance, working tirelessly to safeguard their ancestral lands and uphold their cultural heritage. We strive to amplify the voices of the Lumad, shedding light on their struggles and fostering solidarity. Through grassroots initiatives, legal support, and community empowerment, LumadUprise aims to ensure a future where the Lumad people can live in peace, dignity, and freedom.
